Starting a new project is not easy.

With entrepreneurship at an all time high it is no surprise that you may find yourself with an unusual itch to try something new. That unwavering feeling at the pit of your stomach that tells you to go ahead and jump.

Maybe it’s a side-hustle, or maybe it’s a new opportunity that comes to your doorstep. Whether it’s an idea that you shared with your friends over a holiday weekend or a dream that you long to make come true. One thing is certain…

We can all learn from those who have succeeded at taking their leap and making their dreams come true. This is a list of some of my teams favorite entrepreneurs and their words of wisdom that continue to motivate starting and current entrepreneurs across the globe.

1. “When something is important enough, you do it even if the odds are not in your favor” – Elon Musk

2. “Do the work. Everyone wants to be successful but nobody wants to do the work” – Gary Vaynerchuk

3. “No matter how many mistakes you make or how slow you progress you are still way ahead of everyone who isn’t trying.” – Tony Robbins

4. “Without passion you don’t have energy. Without energy, you have nothing” – Warren Buffet

5. “I’m convinced that about half of what separates SUCCESSFUL ENTREPRENEURS from the non-successful ones is pure perseverance.” – Steve Jobs
